## Five Structural Exposures  
1. **Human Visibility**  
Required disclosure: All human labor involved in system operations must be visible and attributable.  

2. **Capacity Integrity**  
Required disclosure: Systems must maintain operational capacity without dependency on individual vigilance.  

3. **Choice & Representation Rights**  
Required disclosure: Users must have verifiable control over data representation and system interactions.  

4. **Conflict & Competitive Boundaries**  
Required disclosure: Clear demarcation of competitive boundaries to prevent monopolistic practices.  

5. **Power & Responsibility Mapping**  
Required disclosure: Explicit mapping of power dynamics and accountability structures within systems.  

This protocol establishes structural transparency obligations for Paritsea implementations.
